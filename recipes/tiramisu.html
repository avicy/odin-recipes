Tiramisu

Description

Tiramisu is a classic Italian dessert known for it's rich creamy texture and coffee aftertaste. It consists of layers of ladyfinger cookies dipped in espresso, which are then layered with a mixture of whipped eggs, sugar, and mascarpone cheese. The name Tiramisu translates to "pick me up" in Italian.

Ingredients

  • Ladyfinger cookies
  • Mascarpone cheese
  • Eggs
  • Cocoa
  • Coffee

Steps

  1. Whisk egg yolks and sugar: In a bowl, whisk egg yolks and sugar until thick and pale.
  2. Add Mascarpone: Mix in mascarpone cheese until smooth.
  3. Whip Cream:In a separate bowl, whip heavy cream until stiff peaks form.
  4. Combine Mixtures: Gently fold whipped cream into the mascarpone mixture.
  5. Prepare Coffee Mixture: In a shallow dish, combine cooled coffee and coffee liqueur (if using).
  6. Dip Ladyfingers: Quickly dip each ladyfinger into the coffee mixture.
  7. Layer Ladyfingers: Arrange a layer of dipped ladyfingers in the bottom of a dish.
  8. Add Cream Layer: Spread half of the mascarpone mixture over the ladyfingers.
  9. Repeat Layers: Add another layer of dipped ladyfingers and the remaining mascarpone mixture.
  10. Chill and Serve: Cover and refrigerate for at least 4 hours. Dust with cocoa powder before serving.
