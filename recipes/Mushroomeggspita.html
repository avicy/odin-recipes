Mushroom and Eggs Pita

Description

This Mushroom and Eggs Pita is a quick and healthy breakfast option, filled with sautéed mushrooms, scrambled eggs, and a refreshing yogurt sauce.

Ingredients

  • 4 pita breads
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil, divided
  • 8 oz sliced white button mushrooms
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 lemons, juiced
  • 1 oz feta cheese, crumbled
  • ¼ cup Greek yogurt
  • 8 large eggs
  • 1 small red onion, sliced
  • 1-2 cups mixed leafy greens

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C) and warm the pita breads for 8-10 minutes.
  2. In a bowl, marinate mushrooms with olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  3. Prepare the yogurt sauce by blending Greek yogurt with lemon juice and basil.
  4. Scramble the eggs with feta cheese in a sauté pan with garlic and mushrooms.
  5. Stuff the warm pita with scrambled eggs, mushrooms, greens, and drizzle with yogurt sauce.
