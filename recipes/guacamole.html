Guacamole with Tortillas

Description

This delicious guacamole is made with fresh avocados and served with crispy baked tortilla chips. It's a perfect appetizer or snack for any occasion!

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe Haas avocados
  • 2 tablespoons key lime juice
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons finely chopped serrano pepper
  • ½ cup chopped Roma tomato
  • 6 tablespoons chopped onion
  • 6 tablespoons chopped cilantro
  • 8 corn tortillas (for chips)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C) and slice the tortillas into quarters.
  2. Arrange the tortilla wedges on a parchment-lined baking sheet and spray with coconut oil.
  3. Bake for 10-20 minutes until crispy, flipping halfway through.
  4. In a bowl, mash the avocados with lime juice and salt.
  5. Fold in the serrano pepper, tomato, onion, and cilantro into the mashed avocado.
  6. Serve the guacamole with the baked tortilla chips.
